DEVKIT-MPC5744P board: Simple LED and PLL usage Example
*Configures CLKOUT signal and PLL to 160 MHz
*Divides PLL by 10 for output frequency of 16 MHz
*Configures  CLKOUT signal to 16 MHz. 
*Connects to LED
*LED flashes at frequency of CLKOUT
*Onboard red RGB turns on to let user know program is running, but does not flash.
