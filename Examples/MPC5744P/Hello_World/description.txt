DEVKIT-MPC5744P board: Simple LED usage Example
*Sets the default clock source is 16 MHz IRCOSC
*Divides IRCOSC by 10 for output frequency of 1.6 MHz
*Configures CLKOUT signal to 1.6 MHz
*Connects to LED
*LED flashes at frequency of CLKOUT
*Onboard red RGB turns on to let user know program is running, but does not flash.
